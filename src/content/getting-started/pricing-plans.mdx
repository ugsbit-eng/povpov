## Free Tier: Learn & Test

**Perfect for:**
- New users learning automated trading
- Testing strategies with small amounts
- Evaluating P.O.V Sniper BOT before committing

**What you get:**
- 1 active strategy at a time
- Limited to 3 trading pairs (e.g., SOL/USDC, RAY/USDC, BONK/USDC)
- Basic grid and DCA trading
- Raydium DEX only
- 30 days of trade history
- Community support via Discord

**Limitations:**
- No sniper mode (for new token launches)
- No custom strategy builder
- Basic POV analysis (price only, no orderflow/volume)
- No backtesting
- Lower priority on shared RPC (may experience delays during high congestion)

**How to start:**
1. [Sign up for free account](/signup)
2. Connect wallet
3. Create your first strategy
4. Start trading!

**Upgrade prompt**: You'll see upgrade suggestions when trying to access Pro features.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch plans mid-month?**
A: Yes. Upgrading is instant with prorated charge. Downgrading takes effect at next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I exceed Free tier limits?**
A: Bot stops executing new trades. You'll see upgrade prompt. Upgrade to resume.

**Q: Do you offer lifetime plans?**
A: No. Our infrastructure costs (RPC, servers) are ongoing.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Not currently. Cancel and re-subscribe when ready. (Pausing feature coming Q2 2025)

**Q: What if I only need it for 1 week?**
A: Monthly plan is minimum. But with 7-day refund, you can try risk-free.

**Q: Is there a trading volume fee?**
A: No. Trade as much as you want. Plans are flat-rate.
